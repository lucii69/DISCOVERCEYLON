Ella is a beautiful hill-country town in Sri Lanka, well known for its scenic hiking trails and cool climate. Surrounded by tea plantations, mountains, and waterfalls, Ella attracts nature lovers and adventure seekers from around the world.

Popular hikes in Ella include Little Adam’s Peak and Ella Rock, both offering breathtaking panoramic views of valleys and green landscapes. These trails vary in difficulty, making them suitable for beginners as well as experienced hikers. Early morning hikes are especially rewarding due to misty views and sunrise scenery.

Hiking in Ella is not only about adventure but also about enjoying peace and natural beauty. The fresh air, quiet surroundings, and stunning viewpoints make Ella an ideal destination for relaxation, exploration, and unforgettable outdoor experiences.
