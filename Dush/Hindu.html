Koneswaram Temple – Trincomalee


Ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva
Located on Swami Rock with breathtaking ocean views
One of the Pancha Ishwarams (five sacred Shiva temples)

Munneswaram Temple – Chilaw


Important temple dedicated to Lord Shiva
Visited by both Hindus and Buddhists
Known for the Munneswaram annual festival

Naguleswaram Temple – Keerimalai (Jaffna)


Another sacred Pancha Ishwaram of Lord Shiva
Located near the famous Keerimalai sacred water springs
Calm and spiritual environment

Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil – Jaffna


One of the most important Hindu temples in Sri Lanka
Dedicated to Lord Murugan (Kataragama Deviyo)
Famous for the annual Nallur Festival
