Kitulgala — White-Water Rafting & Jungle Adventures


Famous for white-water rafting on the Kelani River,
Kitulgala is a prime spot for rafting, canyoning, jungle trekking and
even zip-lining through rainforest canopies

Yala National Park — Wildlife Safari Adventure


Go on thrillingjeep safaris through Yala to spot leopards, elephants, crocodiles and diverse wildlife across open plains and forests.

Ella — Hiking & Zip-lining in the Hill Country


Ella offers stunning hikes like Ella Rock and Little Adam’s Peak, plus adrenaline rushes on the Flying Ravana zipline soaring over tea estates and valleys.

Horton Plains National Park — Hikes to World’s End


A must-visit for outdoor lovers: trek through misty plains to World’s End, a dramatic cliff with breathtaking views, and explore trails like Baker’s Falls
