1. Sri Maha Bodhi – Anuradhapura

One of the oldest living trees in the world

Grown from a sapling of the Bodhi tree under which Buddha attained enlightenment

A UNESCO World Heritage Site


2. Ruwanwelisaya – Anuradhapura

One of the largest and most sacred stupas in Sri Lanka

Built by King Dutugemunu

Beautiful white dome, very peaceful atmosphere


3. Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa) – Kandy

Houses the Sacred Tooth Relic of Lord Buddha

One of the most important Buddhist temples in the world

Located near the scenic Kandy Lake



4. Mihintale – Anuradhapura

Known as the birthplace of Buddhism in Sri Lanka

Where Arahat Mahinda met King Devanampiyatissa

Famous for its stone steps and panoramic views



5. Dambulla Cave Temple (Golden Temple) – Dambulla

A UNESCO World Heritage Site

Contains 150+ Buddha statues inside cave temples

Amazing ancient wall paintings and rock caves



6. Abhayagiri Monastery – Anuradhapura

One of the largest monastery complexes of ancient Sri Lanka

Important center of Buddhist learning

Massive stupa with historical ruins around
